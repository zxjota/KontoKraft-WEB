KontoKraft — Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 23, 2025

Welcome to KontoKraft ("we", "us", or "our"). Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information when you use the KontoKraft mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when you use the Service, including:

  • Account Information: email address, display name, password (when you create an account).
  • Financial Entries: daily ledger entries, transaction descriptions, amounts, categories, dates, photos or attachments you upload, and notes.
  • Support Communications: messages, feedback, or other correspondence you send to us.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Service, we may automatically collect certain data, such as:

  • Usage Data: features used, screens viewed, actions taken, timestamps, and crash reports.
  • Device Information: device model, operating system and version, device identifiers, mobile network information, and app version.
  • Log Data: technical logs and diagnostic information to help us troubleshoot and improve the Service.

1.3 Location & Other Optional Data

If you grant permission, we may collect approximate location data to support location-based features. You remain in control of location permissions via your device settings.

1.4 Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to provide and improve the Service, remember user preferences, and enable analytics. Most cookies are necessary for basic functionality; you can manage cookie settings via your device or browser, though disabling some cookies may affect functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, maintain, and improve the Service;
  • To process and store your daily accounting and inventory records;
  • To respond to your requests and provide customer support;
  • To send important notices and administrative messages;
  • To analyze usage patterns, conduct research, and improve features;
  • To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, security incidents, or other harmful activity;
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies and terms of service.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may disclose information in limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: trusted third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics, crash reporting, payment processing). They only access data necessary to provide their services and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: when required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of KontoKraft or others.
  • Business Transfers: in connection with an organizational change such as a merger, acquisition, reorganization, sale, or bankruptcy—subject to confidentiality protections.
  • With Your Consent: when you expressly request or authorize sharing (for example, exporting your data to an external accounting service).

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, regulatory compliance).

5. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. While we strive to protect your information, no system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will comply with applicable legal requirements to notify you and relevant authorities.

6. Analytics, Advertising & Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Firebase, Google Analytics, or similar) to analyze usage and improve the app. These providers may collect certain information about your use of the Service. We do not use personal data for advertising purposes without your consent. Please review third-party privacy policies for details about their data practices.

7. Your Choices & Rights

Subject to local law, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you;
  • Correction: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • Deletion: request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal exceptions);
  • Portability: request a machine-readable copy of the data you provided;
  • Restriction or Objection: request restriction of processing or object to certain uses;
  • Opt-out: opt out of promotional communications at any time via unsubscribe links or by contacting us.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We may require identity verification before processing certain requests.

8. Children’s Privacy

KontoKraft is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you learn that your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove the information.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be stored and processed in countries other than where you live. When we transfer personal data internationally, we take reasonable steps to ensure it is handled securely and consistent with this Privacy Policy and applicable law (for example, by using contractual safeguards).
